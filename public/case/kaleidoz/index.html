About

I’m Chris Ross Harris, I’m a Designer and Photographer based in Louisville, Kentucky specializing in Branding and Visual Design Systems for projects like apps, games, installations and websites using technologies like Sketch, Adobe, HTML, CSS, WebGL, GLSL and JavaScript.

I’m working at LEAP Group Network as a Visual Designer.
I'm most known for my work with L.C. King Mfg. Co. and White Stone Oyster Co. In the past, I’ve also collaborated with WORK Labs and OpenEye.

During my career, I have had the opportunity to collaborate in challenging projects for renown brands such as Hamilton Beach, VCU Brandcenter, and Wellcraft Mfg.
